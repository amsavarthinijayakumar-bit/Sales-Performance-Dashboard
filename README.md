# Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
Source: Kaggle
This project analyzes retail sales data using Microsoft Excel. The dataset was cleaned and transformed using Excel functions, sorting, filtering, and Pivot Tables.

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Slicers
* Data Cleaning

### Dashboard Features

 * KPI Cards — Total Sales, Total Profit, Average Profit at a glance
 * Sales & Profit by Category and Sub-Category
 * State and City Performance breakdown
 * Year-Month trend charts
 * Interactive Slicers for dynamic filtering by year, category, and region

### Key Insights

### Overall Performance


Total Sales: $6,182,639
Total Profit: $1,610,697
Average Profit per Transaction: $1,348.99
Profit Margin: ~26% — healthy margin across the business


### Category Performance

* Highest Category Profit of Office Supplies $551,575 
* Office Supplies is the top profit-generating category despite not always leading in sales volume
* This highlights that higher sales ≠ higher profit — margin matters more than volume


### Sub-Category Performance

* Highest Sub-Category Profit of Markers $174,749
* Markers alone contributed significantly to overall profitability
* Low-cost, high-margin stationery items drive the most profit


### Geographic Performance

* Top state Profit Florida (State) $308,706 
* Florida leads in profit while New York leads in sales — a key strategic insight
* This divergence suggests New York has lower margins possibly due to higher operational costs
* Orlando is the single most profitable city, contributing $128,125 in profit alone

### Business Recommendations


* Prioritize Florida and Orlando for expansion — highest profit margins
* Investigate New York — high sales but lower profitability suggests pricing or cost issues
* Focus inventory on Markers and Office Supplies — highest return on investment
